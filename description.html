App Description

*MAKING A BEAUTIFUL LOGO HAS NEVER BEEN EASIER*

Logo Maker Shop makes it super easy to create a stunning logo for your business in seconds.

Logo design work could not be any simpler than this:
1. Choose from pre-made 1000+ logo templates created by professional designers.
2. Edit with our 5000+ font, symbol, and background design resources.

The best part is that you do not have to start everything from scratch. Just pick one from our template collection and get creative.

* 1000+ PRE-MADE LOGO TEMPLATES:
Logo Shop includes a variety of beautiful logo designs from simple ones to more elaborate combinations. Our pre-made logo collection has 13 categories (Basic, Badge, Iconic, Letter-Based, Round, Initial Based, Vintage, Doodle, Animal, Abstract, Colorful, Handwriting, Cutie). All elements of each logo designs are fully adjustable.

* 5000+ DESIGN RESOURCES
Logo Shop has an excellent collection of 5000+ design resources of fonts, symbols, shapes, icons, and backgrounds. Each resource is carefully chosen and crafted to make your logo stand out.

* SAVE YOUR LOGOS AND EDIT ANYTIME AND ANYWHERE
Logo Shop is a Universal(iPhone/iPad) app with iCloud support. Whenever you find inspiration, just open the app and tweak your logo. It will be shared between your devices using iCloud.

* AUTO-SAVING FEATURE
Lost your logo work? Logo Shop auto-saves your editing and will ask you to load the work when you re-launches the app.
* UNDO/REDO
Made a mistake while editing? No problem! You can just undo/redo everything step of your logo editing.
* High-Resolution EXPORT
Save and share your logos as a PNG or JPEG image. Transparent PNG image can be saved up to 2048 x 2048 resolution.
